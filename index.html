Bumpabag



The Bumpabag is a soft shell bag which extends the storage capacity of small cars.It is easy to install and is far cheaper than the alternatives of roof boxes or trailers.Whether you're going camping for the weekend, moving back to uni or going to a festival with friends, the Bumpabag will solve your storage issues.

The Bumpabag is the result of a 3rd year manufacturing engineering project by Grace Copplestone, Guy Simon and Cameron Smith. Developed at the Institute of Manufacturing, the Bumpabag is still a prototype.

Development

The Bumpabag design is a result of several prototypes, all of which were fabricated by the team. The final product was taken out on the streets of Cambridge. Whilst driving the Bumpabag remains securely fastened to the boot and horozontal straps restrict movement when travelling around corners.

Design features

  • Cut resistant straps
  • Cut proof PVC fabric
  • Eye catching design
  • Three curved, fibre glass struts in the back of the bag
