Bumpabag

This website is under construction, please bear with us.

Bumpabag is a simple product which extends the storage capacity of small cars.It is easy to install and a bargain in comparison to roof boxes or trailers.Whether you're going camping for the weekend, moving back to uni or going to a festival with your friends, the Bumpabag will solve your storage issues.

The Bumpabag is the product of a 3rd year manufacturing engineering project by Grace Copplestone, Guy Simon and Cameron Smith. Developed at the Institute of Manufacturing, the Bumpabag is currently at the prototyping stage. We welcome any feedback, just follow the link to our feedback form

Development

The Bumpabag is the product of a year's work. All the prototypes were manufactured in house and tested on the streets of Cambridge.

Design features

  • Cut resistant straps
  • Cut proof PVC fabric
  • Eye catching design
  • Three curved, fibre glass struts in the back of the bag
